Dress code

Dress code will be Garden Cocktail attire. Although we encourage you to wear what makes you feel fabulous, please refrain from wearing neutrals such as black and white as we invite guests to dress in our color palette below.

For gents, we encourage to wear light suit or pair a sport coat with a linen button-down shirt, pants. Wearing a tie is optional.

For ladies, a formal knee-length (mid/tea-length dress) or summer sundress and jumpsuits that are light and floaty also works well. Avoid high heels and choose something comfortable yet luxurious — like your favorite sandals or flats. If you must wear heels, try a wedge shape instead.
